# Slide 02 Blueprint

## Purpose

Frame the session around one central question and answer the audience's implicit question: why should educators pay attention?

## Core Takeaway

The educational issue is no longer whether AI matters, but how schools should respond to rapidly changing capabilities.

## Include

- A strong framing title.
- The central question: `What has changed, and what does that mean for schools?`

## Visual / Layout Direction

- Keep the slide sparse.
- This can work as a big-question slide with strong typography and substantial white space.

## Speaker Guidance

- Clarify that this is not a science fiction talk.
- Clarify that this is not a talk about replacing teachers.
- Emphasize that the session is about tools and capabilities already affecting teaching, learning, curriculum, and assessment.

## Transition

Lead into a visual explanation of why this moment feels urgent.
