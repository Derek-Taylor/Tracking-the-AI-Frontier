# Slide 11 Blueprint

## Purpose

Show that the pace of change from roughly March 2023 to March 2026 has been unusually fast and visible.

## Core Takeaway

Many educators are relying on a mental model of AI that is already outdated because capability and usability have improved so quickly.

## Include

- The slide title.
- A timeline visual spanning roughly 2023 through 2026.
- Visible categories of progress such as:
  - reasoning,
  - coding assistance,
  - multimodal input and output,
  - long-context processing,
  - workflow automation,
  - polished product integration.
- A short line reinforcing that the issue is not only today's snapshot, but the rate of change.

## Visual / Layout Direction

- The image file should live in this folder as `frontier-progress-timeline.png`.
- Keep the timeline simple and legible from a distance.
- This slide should feel like evidence of acceleration, not an exhaustive product chronology.

## Speaker Guidance

- Emphasize that improvement has not been limited to chat quality.
- Point out that capabilities that felt surprising recently can become normal very quickly.
- Use absolute dates when speaking about the window if needed: roughly 2023 to 2026.

## Transition

Translate the pace of progress into the educational problem of outdated assumptions.
