# Slide 09 Blueprint

## Purpose

Define the term "AI frontier" clearly and quickly so the audience has a usable frame for the rest of the section.

## Core Takeaway

The AI frontier is the leading edge of capability and product development across reasoning, multimodality, autonomy, tool use, and usability.

## Include

- The slide title.
- A central visual built around the phrase `The AI Frontier`.
- Surrounding capability terms such as:
  - reasoning,
  - multimodality,
  - coding,
  - long context,
  - tool use,
  - autonomy,
  - usability.

## Visual / Layout Direction

- Keep the visual clean and conceptual.
- The image file should live in this folder as `ai-frontier-capabilities.png`.
- Avoid turning this into a dense taxonomy slide.

## Speaker Guidance

- Explain that the frontier is the current leading edge, not a fixed club or a single product.
- Emphasize that capabilities at the frontier often move into mainstream tools surprisingly quickly.

## Transition

Move from the concept of the frontier to the organizations currently driving it.
