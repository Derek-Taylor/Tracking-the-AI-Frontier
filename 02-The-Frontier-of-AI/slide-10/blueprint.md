# Slide 10 Blueprint

## Purpose

Introduce the small set of frontier labs shaping much of the visible progress in AI.

## Core Takeaway

A relatively small number of well-resourced labs are pushing capability growth and product deployment at high speed.

## Include

- The slide title.
- The major labs:
  - OpenAI,
  - Google / DeepMind,
  - Anthropic.
- A short phrase under each lab explaining its relevance.
- Optional logos if the final visual treatment calls for them.

## Visual / Layout Direction

- Keep this slide visually restrained.
- A three-column layout or branded card layout would work well.
- If logos are used, keep them secondary to the idea that a few labs are setting the pace.

## Speaker Guidance

- Keep the list narrow and practical rather than exhaustive.
- Note that these labs shape both model capability and the products through which educators encounter AI.
- You may briefly acknowledge other players, but do not let this turn into an industry overview.

## Transition

Shift from who the labs are to what has changed over the last 36 months.
