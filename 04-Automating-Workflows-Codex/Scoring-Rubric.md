## Rubric: *The Last Question* Brief Response

## Original Prompt

Based on your reading of Isaac Asimov's *The Last Question*:

Over the course of the story, the computer evolves from Multivac (a massive machine on Earth) to the Cosmic AC (an entity existing in hyperspace). In a brief response (under one page), explain how the relationship between humans and the computer changes as the computer gets more advanced.

**Total: 6 points possible**

| Category                                                      | 2 Points                                                                                                                                                                                                                | 1 Point                                                                                                                 | 0 Points                                                                                              |
| ------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Understanding of the Changing Human-Computer Relationship** | Clearly explains how the relationship between humans and the computer changes over the course of the story, showing that humans become more dependent on it and eventually less central as the computer surpasses them. | Shows partial understanding of how the relationship changes, but explanation is incomplete, vague, or oversimplified.   | Does not explain how the relationship changes, or response shows major misunderstanding of the story. |
| **Use of Story Evidence / Specific Details**                  | Uses accurate and relevant details from the story (such as Multivac, later forms of AC, human dependence, or the ending) to support the explanation.                                                                    | Includes at least one story detail, but details may be weak, too general, or only loosely connected to the explanation. | Uses no relevant story details, or details are inaccurate.                                            |
| **Clarity and Focus of Writing**                              | Response is clear, organized, and focused on the prompt. Writing is easy to follow and stays within the task.                                                                                                           | Response is somewhat clear but may be disorganized, repetitive, or drift away from the prompt at times.                 | Response is unclear, very disorganized, or does not address the prompt effectively.                   |

## Score Guide

* **6** = Strong understanding, well supported, clearly written
* **4–5** = Adequate understanding, some support, mostly clear
* **2–3** = Limited understanding, weak support, unclear in places
* **0–1** = Minimal or no meaningful response
