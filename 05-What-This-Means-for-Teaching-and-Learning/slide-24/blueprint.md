# Slide 24 Blueprint

## Purpose

Provide the on-screen discussion canvas that stays up while participants think, talk with neighbors, and prepare to share out.

## Core Takeaway

These questions are worth discussing openly because they affect core professional decisions, not just tech policy.

## Include

- A clear invitation to talk with nearby colleagues.
- Four prompt bullets:
  - student thinking to protect,
  - assignments or products that feel less trustworthy,
  - teacher work that participants would want help with,
  - equity, ethics, privacy, or trust concerns.

## Visual / Layout Direction

- This slide should stay up for the discussion period.
- Use large text and generous spacing.
- Consider a four-quadrant layout if the final design supports it.

## Speaker Guidance

- Give participants a short moment to reflect silently first.
- Then invite brief partner talk.
- After that, ask for a few public share-outs.

## Transition

Bring the room back together and move toward the closing professional stance.
