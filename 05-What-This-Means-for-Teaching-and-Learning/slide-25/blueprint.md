# Slide 25 Blueprint

## Purpose

Close the intellectual part of the talk with a clear professional stance.

## Core Takeaway

The durable response to AI in education is thoughtful adaptation: clarity, judgment, revision, and ongoing attention.

## Include

- A closing title focused on thoughtful adaptation.
- Short bullets that reject both panic and denial.
- A final action-oriented line about staying informed and adjusting thoughtfully.

## Visual / Layout Direction

- This should feel like a synthesis slide rather than a dense recap.
- Keep the message simple and memorable.

## Speaker Guidance

- Restore coherence after the discussion.
- End with confidence and professional seriousness rather than hype.

## Transition

Move into the thank-you and survey close.
