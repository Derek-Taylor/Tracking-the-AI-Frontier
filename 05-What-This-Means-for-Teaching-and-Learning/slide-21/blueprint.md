# Slide 21 Blueprint

## Purpose

Create the pivot from the demonstrations to their educational consequences.

## Core Takeaway

The audience should shift from "these tools are impressive" to "these developments have implications for school work."

## Include

- A strong title framed as an educational question.
- Two short lines that make the pivot explicit.

## Visual / Layout Direction

- Keep the slide clean and calm.
- This should feel like a reset slide before the discussion-heavy part of the talk.

## Speaker Guidance

- Make it clear that the point of the demos was to surface capability, not to advocate blind adoption.
- Signal that skepticism and professional judgment are welcome here.

## Transition

Move into the first cluster of classroom implications: student thinking and assessment.
