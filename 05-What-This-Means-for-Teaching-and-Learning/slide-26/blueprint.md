# Slide 26 Blueprint

## Purpose

End the presentation with a clean, intentional final slide that invites participants to complete the survey.

## Core Takeaway

The conversation should continue, and participant reflection matters.

## Include

- A simple thank-you.
- A survey QR code image referenced as `survey-qr.png`.
- A visible survey link placeholder that can be replaced with the real URL later.
- Optional one-line prompt encouraging participants to share their main takeaway.

## Visual / Layout Direction

- Keep this slide warm, uncluttered, and easy to act on quickly.
- The QR code should be large enough to scan from the room.

## Speaker Guidance

- Thank the audience sincerely.
- Encourage them to complete the survey before they leave.
- Optionally invite one final takeaway or reflection.

## Transition

This is the final slide of the presentation.
