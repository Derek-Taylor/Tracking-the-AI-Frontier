# Slide 23 Blueprint

## Purpose

Widen the conversation beyond student use and into teacher workflow, ethics, and institutional response.

## Core Takeaway

AI may help with routine work, but it increases the importance of human judgment and raises real equity and trust questions.

## Include

- A title combining teacher practice, equity, and trust.
- Short bullets about:
  - delegation,
  - human judgment,
  - efficiency vs. wisdom,
  - equity, privacy, bias, and transparency.

## Visual / Layout Direction

- Keep this compact and balanced.
- The goal is breadth without overwhelming the audience.

## Speaker Guidance

- Connect teacher use to fairness, appropriateness, and professional responsibility.
- Make clear that school response is not just a technical issue.

## Transition

Invite the room to process the ideas with nearby colleagues.
